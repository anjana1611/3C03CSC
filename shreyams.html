M.V.Shreyams Kumar

M. V. Shreyams Kumar is a former member of the 12th & 13th Kerala Legislative Assembly, who served from 2006-2016 representing the Kalpetta constituency. He is the son of late M.P Veerendra Kumar, a notable politician from Kerala. Presently, he is the Managing Director of Mathrubhumi newspaper and former member of Janata Dal (United) party.
